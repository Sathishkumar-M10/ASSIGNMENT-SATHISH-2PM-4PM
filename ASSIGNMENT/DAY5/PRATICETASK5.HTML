This is all about Anime

Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity lives behind massive walls to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight back against these terrifying beings, uncovering shocking truths about the Titans and the world itself. With intense battles, emotional character arcs, and themes of freedom and survival, it has become one of the most influential anime of the past decade.
